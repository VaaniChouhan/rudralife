### Beat by Beat
```
0:00–0:01   Pure white screen
            Singing bowl strike, one note held
            Complete stillness

0:01–0:03   White warms and thins
            Himalayan aerial descent begins
            Already moving, already inside it
            Mount Kailash identified ahead
            Zooming toward it specifically

0:03–0:05   Lord Shiva revealed on Kailash
            Photorealistic, full reverence
            Padmasana seated, deep meditation
            Blue-white skin, jata with Ganga
            Crescent moon, Rudraksha mala visible
            Trishul beside him in snow
            Snow settled on shoulders
            Eyes closed, cosmic stillness
            Camera zooming in slowly with reverence
            All three eyes present — closed

0:05–0:07   Zoom into his face
            All eyes visible — regular and third
            Third eye most luminous — held like a breath
            A single tear forms at corner of his eye
            The Shiva Purana version:
            tears of compassion after
            thousand years of meditation
            for the welfare of all beings
            The tear holds — the longest pause
            in the entire sequence

0:07–0:08   The tear falls
            Slow motion, perfectly centered
            Luminous, golden-white
            Ancient Himalayan earth below
            Complete audio silence for this moment

0:08–0:09   Earth → shoot → tree → bead
            Compressed, fast, mythic
            Camera moves through not watching
            Deep blue Rudraksha fruit opens
            Brown bead revealed in first light

0:09–0:10   Match dissolve
            Bead stays centered
            Leaf dissolves, human hands dissolve in
            Himalayan valley behind the hands
            Camera stills completely

0:10        Hero text arrives word by word
            Video breathes slowly behind it
```

### Generation Specs
```
Tool            → Midjourney v6 for Shiva reference frame
                  then Runway Gen-3 or Kling 1.6
                  feed reference frame as starting image

Format          → 9:16 vertical
Camera          → ARRI Alexa anamorphic
Grade           → Kodak Vision3 500T film emulation
Colour          → Cold blue-white for Kailash/Shiva scenes
                  warming to gold as tear falls and tree grows
Movement        → Always forward, never stopping
                  only the tear moment slows

Generate as     → 5 overlapping clips (3 sec each, 1 sec overlap)
Stitch in       → CapCut / Premiere
Transition      → DISSOLVE on each overlap, ease in ease out
```

## 6. TRANSITION GRAMMAR — COMPLETE RULES

```
TYPE 1  Environmental Continuation
        Used between: sections in same emotional zone
        Method: bg dims to 20%, next bg fades up behind
        Duration: 800ms
        Easing: cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)

TYPE 2  Match Cut
        Used when: entering or exiting video sections
        Method: visual element rhymes between sections
                (bead → bead / tree → tree / lamp → lamp)
                the cut is instant — the rhyme is seamless

TYPE 3  Exhale
        Used when: moving from UI section to video section
        Duration: 1200ms total
        Method: UI fades → colour holds 1 second → video emerges

TYPE 4  Grounding
        Used when: moving from video section to UI section
        Duration: 600ms
        Method: video desaturates + scales 5%
                new bg colour fades in as overlay
                UI slides up beneath
```
