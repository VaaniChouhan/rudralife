## Discrepancies and Observations

### 1. Swapped Order: Knowledge Center (18) and Bestselling Books (19)
- **Client request**:
  - Section 18: KNOWLEDGE CENTER ("Learn. Reflect. Grow.")
  - Section 19: BESTSELLING BOOKS ("Go Deeper Into The World Of Rudraksha.")
- **Current code**: Swapped and combined under a single section `s25`.
  - Books are displayed first.
  - Knowledge Center is displayed below the books.

### 2. Missing Optional Sections
- **24. INDRA MALA OFFERED TO LORD SHIVA (OPTIONAL)** is missing.
- **25. CELEBRITIES & INFLUENCERS (OPTIONAL)** is missing.

### 3. Insertions
- **Siddha Mala Showcase** (`s-siddha-mala`) is inserted after Rarest Collection to build immediate high-ticket interest.
- **The Unboxing Experience** (`s13`) is inserted before Exhibitions to build expectation of certified quality and premium unboxing.
- **Every Walk of Life** (`s6`) is inserted before the Final CTA to show community categories (athletes, artists, etc.).
